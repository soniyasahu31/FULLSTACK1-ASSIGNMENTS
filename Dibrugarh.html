Dibrugarh

Dibrugarh is considered the Tea City of India and is the major city in the northeastern region of India. It is the growing communication and industrial hub of North East India. Buridihing is the tributary of the Brahmaputra that divides the district from east to west. Buridihing consists of three tea-producing districts of Tinsukia, Dibrugarh, and Sivasagar.
